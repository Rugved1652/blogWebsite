Acer Nitro 2020 series

acer`s nitro series laptops are entry level gaming laptops which comes with low price and great specs. last year in 2019 nitro series was a big success. Price was cheap and specs were great but it came with some serious flaws like heating issues, display, GPU and CPU shared same heat sink pipe,No thunderbolt port. But this Acer nitro 2020 lineup laptops are pretty improved.

this year acer`s engineers does great work in thermal issues. In last year laptops GPU and CPU were share same heat sink pipe but this year CPU and GPU has separate heat pipes.this change improves cooling and perfomance greatly.Quadflow cooling technology improves thermal stats of this laptop which was missing in earlier models because they shared same pipe. acer Nitro 2020 comes with intel 10th gen and ryzen 4000 series processors. i5-10300H and ryzen models come with this new design and i7 varient has 9th gen(9700H) processor still sticked with 2019 design but it has powerful RTX 2060 when i5 varient come with 10th gen processor/ryzen 4000 and gtx 1650 ti which is very confusing.

If you are buying a gaming laptops then you have to compromize with battery life because heavy GPU eats battery so fast, it has wh battery so battery life is arround 5-7 hours while browsing and 2 hours max while gaming it has 2x usb 3.2 gen 2 ports, 1xhdmi 2.0, 1xkiller 2600 ethernet, 1xtype c with(usb 3.2 gen 2),kingston lock,3.5 mm audio jack and zero thunderbolt port. these laptops are rich in ports but thunderbolt port is still missing.

FHD 144hz display is dilightful treat for gamers and content creators. weight of these laptops are around 2-2.2kg which is good but not good for frequent traveling laptops come with 135W charger. New design laptop has 4 zone rgb keybord,full numpad and microsoft precision trackpad.it has 3 slot for drives 2 nvme.m2 and 1 sata drive and 2 memory slot with maximum capesity of 32 gb of ddr4 ram overall i5 10th gen and RYZEN 4000 models are excellent choice to buy and if you need i7 processor laptop and you dont have budget problem then go with predator or triton series which gives you very same specs. price range of this series starts with $699 to and end at $1199
