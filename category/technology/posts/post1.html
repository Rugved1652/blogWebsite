Qualcomm launched 5G midrange processor

All you need to know about Qualcomm`s latest chipset snapdragon 690 is given below.

Qualcomm launched new 6 series processor snapdragon 690 with 5G support.This processor comes with X51 5G Modem-RF System which is Qualcomm`s midrange 5G modem. early this year Qualcomm launched 7 and 8 series processor 765G and 865 with 5g support.It supports multisim enable connections at worlwide level and global bands SA and NSA.

snapdragon 690 is successor of snapdragon 675 and comes with Krayo 560 CPU and Adreno 619L gpu. Adreno 619L GPU provide 60% perfomance improvement and Kryo 560 CPU gives you 20% more perfomance than snapdragon 675. snapdragon 690 gives very good graphic rendering with new Adreno 619L.This chipset comes with 5th gen AI engine and gives you 70% more improvement in AI perfomance comparinng to its predecessor.

snapdragon 690 comes with many good features.snapdragon 690 is based on 8mm process technology so we can see very good power efficiency.first time we can record 4K HDR video in 6 series processors It supports single camera maximum 192MP, spectra 355L Image signal processor, and slow motion support at 720p at 240 Frame per second which are really impresive camera features. this chipset can support display at FHD+ resolution with 120Hz refresh rate and QHD resolution at 60Hz refresh rate. we can charge Snapdragon 690 phone with Quickcharge 4+ if your handset company enabled this feature. features like 5G, NFC, NavIC, bluetooth 5.1,WiFi 6(802.11.ax) these connectivity and location support makes you futureproof. you need WiFi 6 Routers and Devices to harness maximum speed of WiFi 6.

Recently Reliance announce Jio is 5G ready and waiting for Spectrum Licence in India and also announce that Jio will launch budget friendly 5G smartphone ,Recently Qualcomm Bought 0.15% stake in Jio, So ththis ese events clearly point that we can saw A midrange 5G smartphone from Jio with 690 processor.qualcomm has no rival in midrange segment with their new 5G chipset snapdragon 690.
